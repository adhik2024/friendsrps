How. to. play?

This is like regular rock paper scissors but with a friendly twist. Along with the rules of traditional rock paper scissors, here are the additional rules:

  1. Fire beats paper and scissors
  2. Rock and water balloon beat fire
  3. Water balloon beats fire and paper
  4. Scissors and rock beats water balloon

You might think the game is silly but your point of view is a moo point anyways!
